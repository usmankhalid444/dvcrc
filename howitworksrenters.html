          how it works

          The process is simple. Create an account and tell us about you and your vacation needs.
          We have two ways to help you gain a reservation at a DVC Resort:
          1 - Purchase a current resevations held by members.
          or
          2 - Create a custom request. Pay a per point fee for just what you want. You tell us exactally what you want and just pay for what you use.

          DVC Members do not pay the front desk cash for their rooms. The currency they use is Vacation Club Points. Each Disney Vacation Club resort has a rate chart and members pay by the night based on the room size, view, location, etc. Based on the number required for that stay will determine the amout of money need to pay that member for the vacation. You will be able to chat with the Member renting you the points. They can also make arrange for Magical Express if you are staying on WDW property.
